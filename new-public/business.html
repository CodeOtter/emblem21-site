Armaments Manufacturing as Stable Growth

At the core of Emblem 21 is a vision of how to tame the multi-domain concerns of unrestricted robotic warfare.  We believe our approach can be used to redefine the business cycle of global economics.  Our friends at Arus Defence have begun to use our ideas to power their business models.

Cryptocurrency and Space

As cryptocurrencies accelerate in usage, governments around the world are attempting to regulate them.  CubeCab is helping us explore how to cheaply put miners and transaction nodes into space, outside of terrestrial tax law, to help create a satellite-based Cayman Islands.

This initiative can help bring financial interests into the space sector beyond the military and telecommunications industries, dramatically improving the availability and profitability of future robotic labor and asteroid mining.

Privatized Unemployment

With an abundance of robotic labor, people who cannot find employment due to physical proximity can access opportunities abroad without migration.  Nation states no longer have to bear the political and cultural liabilities of opportunity responsibility while maintaining their tax revenues.

The political and cultural threats of rural labor shortages and urban labor surpluses can be mitigated under this model.
