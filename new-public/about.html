About us

A thought experiment is the seed that contains a forest.

"Create a new emotion."

That was the thought experiment that started Emblem 21.  Imagine a future where a powerful artificial intelligence, capable of improving itself exponentially, could take control of global markets, political elections, and economic signals that drive human economic behavior.  Now imagine this being done in a way where the artificial intelligence could hide its influence on those factors while disguising that influence as genuine human activity.  To do this, the AI would need a working approximation of human emotion.  What would such a future look like?

How would culture, politics, finance, and civics be altered in such a world?

Since 2005, the team at Emblem 21 has been building the network of talent capable of experimenting, understanding, and solving the challenges for such a unique scenario.  When we incorporated, we initially worked on ways to integrate autonomous labor into Californian landfills, Kazakhstani wheat fields, dense urban areas, and Ecuadorian jungles. We discovered the biggest impedence to robotic growth was not technological.  It was the price differences of labor between countries made possible by floating rate currency.  By exploring how a AI could understand human emotion, we discovered a comprehensive way to alter the structure of floating rate currencies to promote self-reinforcing incentives for robotic labor.  Our think tank is designed to refine these discoveries and turn them into policy and action using a variety of multi-polar actors and their domains of influence.
