The Berkeley Business Law Journal (BBLJ) is a Berkeley Law student-run organization that publishes an annual print journal, a blog, and hosts events related to business law.  BBLJ works closely with the Berkeley Center for Law, Business and the Economy (BCLBE) to further enhance our presence nationwide.

BBLJ was started in 2004 by a few Berkeley Law students who wanted the opportunity to learn and engage with business law topics while in law school. Since then, BBLJ has grown into one of the largest journals at Berkeley Law, and for 2013-2014, was ranked the #1 commercial law journal in the country by Expresso.  In 2010, in combination with BCLBE, the journal started The Network, a blog dedicated to financial regulation, financing innovation, and capital markets.  BBLJ also hosts a variety of events each year on the Berkeley Campus, culminating in the Women in Business Law event.

For information regarding the print journal, The Network, or Business Law events at Berkeley Law, please contact bblj@law.berkeley.edu.
