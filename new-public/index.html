Robotic Workforces

Bringing socioeconomic perspectives to robotics.

Robotic labor redefines the economic, financial, political, and cultural relationships for the entire world.  Our think tank provides guidance on how to accelerate robotic workforce integration at the national level to rejuvenate economies, stabilize societies, and restore growth

Growth in the 21st Century

Robotic labor will change how nearly every factor of global finance is calculated

Fix Labor Shortages

Rural areas cannot flourish without labor. The costs and risks of destabilizing immigration policies can be mitigated with a human-piloted robotic workforce.

Empower the Working Class

Surplus labor can be deployed to other nations without voting with their feet.  Tame youth unemployment by converting their gaming habits into productive labor, enhancing taxable income and political stability.

Balance Sovereign Debt

Infrastructure spending, mass labor mobilization, and robot-on-robot warfare allows nations to establish their own military-industrial complex as a new growth-oriented foundation for the world.


It's Not Just Technology

Robotic workforces requires more than just sophisticated machine learning and cutting-edge manufacturing.  We've focused on how to reform the political, academic, cultural, and legal domains to normalize robotic labor.
