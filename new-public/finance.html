Infrastructure Financing

The need for communications and manufacturing infrastructure will mirror the demand for robotic workforces from military, business, and civic sectors.  The growth generated from mitigating labor imbalances, robotic consumption, and commoditizing space labor can more than pay off infrastructure investment and can even be used as model for debt forgiveness between nations.

Cryptocurrency Futures

Our cryptocurrency constellation initiative can create competition in the tax haven arena and indirectly drive private finance and public sectors back into space investment.  There are no easily enforceable laws that bars or taxes extraterrestrial decentralized autonomous organizations or their assets.

To maintain that tax haven, our miners and transaction satellites can convert their future performance into subsidies at launch time for military, telecommunication, and infrastructure launches, guaranteeing the lowest launch price across the world.

Restructuring Gross Domestic Product

As robotic workforces become more abundant, we will fundamentally alter the availability and pricing of previously inaccessible opportunities, primarily in the security, civil, and space sectors.  We believe our approach can drive down the investment and maintenance costs of asteroid mining to the point where we will have more economic opportunities than we have robots or humans.
